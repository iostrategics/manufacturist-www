What is Manufacturist?

An Industry 4.0 podcast for small manufacturers.

Audience:
SME business owners and managers.

Episode format:
1 hour interview with expert(s); not dependent on other episodes.

Subject:
Using simple, low-cost digital tools to stay profitable and competitive — earning more money with existing people and machines.

1. Schedule your 30-minute intro call.

  • This call will be online-only; stable Internet is required.
  • Audio will be recorded automatically in your browser.

What happens during the interview?

The interview will be casual like a friendly conversation.

  • This call will NOT be live.
  • We will be able to edit/cut problem areas.
  • We can freely chat before/after the interview questions.
  • The "official" start/end of the interview will be verbally indicated.

For business owners and managers...

You will be introduced and then asked to explain your role and what your business offers. We'll then cover a roughly chronological experience of the tools you've implemented, what succeeded and failed, and lessons learned that you can pass on to our listeners.

For experts, academics, government officials, vendors, etc...

You will be introduced and asked to explain your role and what your organization does. We'll then cover the topic agreed upon during our intro call.

What happens after the interview?

Your episode will air a few weeks later, depending on our publication schedule.

  • Your interview will be professionally edited.
  • You will sound awesome.
  • You will receive a shareable link to your episode page.

Your episode page will have:

  • A built-in audio player.
  • Links to open in popular podcast apps.
  • A professional (human) transcript.
  • A link back to your organization, valuable for search engine rankings.

Thank you!
